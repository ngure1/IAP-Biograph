Biography

Education

Arun Rupesh Maini was born on 24 October 1995, in Nottingham, England. He attended Nottingham High School and later pursued his higher education at the University of Warwick in Coventry, England. During his time at the university, he focused on economics.

Employment History

While studying at the University of Warwick, Arun had an internship at Pricewaterhouse Coopers, an accounting firm in London. Although the internship was in the field of economics, Arun found it mundane and boring. During this period, he started creating videos about technology on YouTube, and his channel gained popularity.
